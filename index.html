About Me

I’m a full-stack software developer with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from the University of New Haven, combining a strong technical foundation with hands-on experience building web applications, VR simulations, and AI-powered tools. My work spans a range of technologies, from front-end frameworks like Vue.js and React to back-end systems using Flask, Node.js, SQL, and NoSQL databases. I’ve led development on several impactful projects, including a web-based VR platform for football film analysis and a mobile app for automating household chores. Professionally, I’ve contributed as a Data Analyst in the financial sector and as a Research Assistant developing educational games. I thrive in Agile environments, enjoy clean, efficient code, and bring strong communication and leadership skills to every team I join. Outside of tech, I’m an avid golfer who values focus, patience, and continuous improvement traits I bring both to the course and to my work as a developer.
